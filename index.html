About Penguins

Penguins are flightless birds that live primarily in the Southern Hemisphere. They are known for their distinctive black and white plumage and their incredible swimming abilities.

Interesting Facts

  • There are 18 species of penguins, each with unique characteristics.
  • Penguins can swim at speeds of up to 15 miles per hour.
  • They communicate through vocalizations and body language.
